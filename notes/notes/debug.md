# Fixing hidden coordinates + chunk borders not working
In newer versions of Minecraft (roughly since ~1.21.9), there may be issues where you cannot access your in-game coordinates. This is the result of a simple client setting that can be toggled back on.

## How to fix it

### 1. Disable "Reduced Debug Info"
Open your in-game menu, go to Options -> Accessibility Settings. Disable "Reduced Debug Info" and save.
